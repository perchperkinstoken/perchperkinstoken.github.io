How To Buy $PERKS

01
Set Up a Web3 Wallet
Download a secure, reputable Web3 browser extension or mobile wallet. Generate your private key phrase, secure it offline, and make sure to add or switch your network to the Arbitrum Layer-2 chain.
02
Deposit Native Gas Tokens
Acquire Ethereum (ETH) from your preferred cryptocurrency exchange platform. Withdraw the funds directly to your wallet address using the Arbitrum network option to cover minimal transaction network fees.
03
Connect to a DEX & Swap
Navigate to a supported decentralized exchange (DEX) operating on Arbitrum. Connect your active wallet, paste the official $PERKS token contract address, and execute your swap using your deposited tokens.
04
Import Token Contract
If your tokens do not appear automatically after the transaction clears, go to your wallet dashboard, select "Import Custom Token," and paste the verified token address to monitor your balance.
